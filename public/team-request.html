1. Hero Section Media Required

The hero section is the first thing visitors see on the homepage. We want to make it impactful with a background video.

Background Video

A short promotional or highlight video that will play automatically (muted) in the background of the hero section.

Video Specifications:

  • Format: MP4 (H.264 codec) - best browser compatibility
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) maximum
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Duration: 15-30 seconds (will loop continuously)
  • File Size: Keep under 10-15 MB for fast loading
  • Content: Event highlights, worship moments, past conference clips, or promo video

Poster Image (Fallback)

A static image shown while the video loads, or on mobile devices to save bandwidth.

Image Specifications:

  • Format: JPG or WebP
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • File Size: Under 500 KB (optimized for web)
  • Content: Should match/complement the video - could be a frame from the video or event photo
Note: The video will be hosted on Firebase Storage with caching enabled for optimal performance. The image will display instantly while the video loads in the background.

4. Conference Booklet (Digital Copy) Required

A digital copy of the conference booklet for the Downloads section. This is useful for attendees who prefer to use a tablet when taking notes - they can use Adobe Acrobat Reader to annotate and take notes directly on the digital copy.

File Specifications:

  • Format: PDF
  • Recommended Size: Under 10 MB for easy download
  • Features: Should be selectable text (not scanned images) for better accessibility
Tip: Attendees can open the PDF in Adobe Acrobat Reader on their tablet, highlight text, add notes, and save their annotated copy - making it a paperless option for taking session notes.
