1. Hero Section Media Required

The hero section is the first thing visitors see on the homepage. We want to make it impactful with a background video.

Background Video

A short promotional or highlight video that will play automatically (muted) in the background of the hero section.

Video Specifications:

  • Format: MP4 (H.264 codec) - best browser compatibility
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) maximum
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Duration: 15-30 seconds (will loop continuously)
  • File Size: Keep under 10-15 MB for fast loading
  • Content: Event highlights, worship moments, past conference clips, or promo video

Poster Image (Fallback)

A static image shown while the video loads, or on mobile devices to save bandwidth.

Image Specifications:

  • Format: JPG or WebP
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • File Size: Under 500 KB (optimized for web)
  • Content: Should match/complement the video - could be a frame from the video or event photo
Current Implementation: The hero section currently uses a gradient background as a placeholder. Once the video/image is provided, it will replace the gradient.

12. Custom Domain Setup (DNS Configuration) Action Required

To make the IDMC 2026 website accessible at idmc-gcfsm.org, we need to update the DNS records in your domain provider's settings. Please coordinate with whoever manages the domain's DNS settings (likely through your domain registrar).

DNS Records to ADD:

Add the following records to your DNS provider to point the domain to Firebase Hosting:

Record Type Domain Name Value
A idmc-gcfsm.org 199.36.158.100
TXT idmc-gcfsm.org hosting-site=idmc-gcfsm
Why is this needed?
  • The A record points your domain to Firebase's servers
  • The TXT record verifies that you own the domain
  • Once configured, idmc-gcfsm.org will show the IDMC 2026 website
  • Firebase will automatically provision an SSL certificate for HTTPS

Common DNS Providers:

Your domain DNS might be managed through:

  • GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, Cloudflare, or your web hosting provider
  • DNS changes typically take 24-48 hours to propagate worldwide
