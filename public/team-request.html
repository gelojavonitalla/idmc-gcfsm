1. Hero Section Media
Required

The hero section is the first thing visitors see on the homepage. We want to make it impactful with a background video.

Background Video

A short promotional or highlight video that will play automatically (muted) in the background of the hero section.

Video Specifications:

  • Format: MP4 (H.264 codec) - best browser compatibility
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) maximum
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Duration: 15-30 seconds (will loop continuously)
  • File Size: Keep under 10-15 MB for fast loading
  • Content: Event highlights, worship moments, past conference clips, or promo video

Poster Image (Fallback)

A static image shown while the video loads, or on mobile devices to save bandwidth.

Image Specifications:

  • Format: JPG or WebP
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • File Size: Under 500 KB (optimized for web)
  • Content: Should match/complement the video - could be a frame from the video or event photo
Current Implementation: The hero section currently uses a gradient background as a placeholder. Once the video/image is provided, it will replace the gradient.

12. SMS Notifications - Sender ID Registration
Required

The website can send SMS notifications to attendees for registration and payment confirmations. To enable this feature, we need to register a Sender ID with our SMS provider (OneWaySMS).

What is a Sender ID?

A Sender ID is the name that appears as the sender when an SMS is received. Instead of seeing a random phone number, recipients will see a branded name like "GCFSM".

Example: When an attendee receives an SMS confirmation, they will see:
From: GCFSM
"Hi Juan! Your IDMC 2026 registration is received. ID: IDMC-ABC123..."

Why is Sender ID Important?

  • Brand Recognition - Recipients immediately know the SMS is from GCF South Metro
  • Trust & Legitimacy - Branded messages appear more trustworthy than random phone numbers
  • Professional Image - Conveys a professional, established organization

Where is Sender ID Used?

The Sender ID will appear on all SMS notifications sent by the website:

  • Registration Confirmation - When an attendee successfully registers
  • Payment Confirmation - When payment is verified and confirmed
  • Payment Reminders - Reminder SMS for pending payments

How to Register a Sender ID

To register a Sender ID with OneWaySMS, we need to email the provider with the following details:

Required Information Value Status
Sender ID GCFSM Proposed
SMS Content Sample: "Hi [Name]! Your IDMC 2026 registration is received..." Ready
Company Name GCF South Metro, Inc. Please verify
Business Permit Copy of valid business permit (required by provider) Needed

Note: Sender ID registration takes 2-3 business days to process.

Action Required: Please provide a copy of the business permit so we can submit the Sender ID registration to OneWaySMS. Without this, SMS notifications will show a generic phone number instead of "GCFSM".
